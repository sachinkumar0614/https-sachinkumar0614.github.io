FAQs

What is Assemble?

Assemble is a tech conference focused on innovation and emerging technologies. It brings together industry leaders, experts, and enthusiasts to explore topics such as AI, blockchain, and more through keynotes, workshops, and networking opportunities.

Who is Assemble for?

Assemble is designed for anyone interested in technology and innovation, including professionals, entrepreneurs, students, and thought leaders. Whether you’re looking to learn, network, or share ideas, there’s something for everyone.

How can I register?

Click one of the register buttons on our website. Tickets are free—we can’t wait to see you there.

Will there be time to network?

Yes, Assemble offers various networking opportunities, including designated networking hours, breakout sessions, and interactive workshops. You’ll have ample chance to connect with fellow attendees and industry leaders.

Will sessions be recorded?

Yes, all registered attendees will get access to session recordings after the conference. This allows you to revisit the insights shared by speakers and catch any sessions you may have missed.
